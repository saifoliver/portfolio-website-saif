Projects

These projects represent how I think and work – not just the final result, but the problem, the approach, and the impact.

Operations Concept – Automotive Import Strategy

Goal: Explore a niche strategy for importing exclusive American vehicles into India.

Approach: Researched customer segments, logistics, regulatory considerations, and brand positioning. Built a conceptual operations and supply chain model focusing on limited-volume, high-value imports.

Outcome: Created a structured concept that can be developed into a more detailed business and operations plan, with clear risks and opportunities identified.

Engineering Leadership Portfolio

Goal: Build a leadership-focused portfolio capturing systems thinking, communication, ethics, and career planning tools.

Approach: Designed a website, organized deliverables into clear sections, and wrote reflections to connect theory to practice.

Outcome: Demonstrated growth in leadership readiness and created a resource I can reuse for future roles.

Applied Statistics Case Study

Goal: Use real data to support a management decision.

Approach: Collected sample data, applied descriptive statistics and hypothesis testing, and translated results into simple recommendations for non-technical stakeholders.

Outcome: Strengthened my ability to interpret results instead of just running calculations, and to communicate uncertainty honestly.
